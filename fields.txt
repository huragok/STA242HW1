place: PLACE, Place - the rank based on the net time
div/total: DIV /TOT, Div /Tot, Div/Tot, Div  /Tot - age division place/number in division
name: NAME, Name - just name
number: NUM, Num - Bib number, can be used as an index
age: AG, Ag - just age
hometown: HOMETOWN, Hometown - country for non-us citizens, city and state for us citizens
gun time: GUN TIM, GUN, Gun, Gun Tim
net time: NET TIM, NET, Net, Net Tim, TIME, Time
pace: PACE, Pace
S: S - seeded runners
split: Split - split time at a certain checkpoint?
5 mile: 5 Mi, 5 Mile - 5 mile check point?
pace for 5 mile: Pace - right after the 5 mile field
10 km: 10 Km - 10 km check point?
